Design, Build, Ship · Assignment 1

Iceland
Travel Diary

25 landing page iterations · February 2026

A rapid design exploration of a single concept — an Iceland travel diary told through drone photography and night sky chasing. Each version asks: what else could this be?

Phase 1 · v1–v10

Go Wide

Radical exploration. Ten completely different visual languages for the same content — from brutalism to vintage journals, cinematic darkness to photo mosaics. The goal: surprise yourself.

v1

Clean Minimal

Single column, Georgia serif, maximum whitespace. The diary as a quiet reading experience. Every other version pushes against this.
v2

Dark Cinematic

Full-bleed hero, near-black background, film-diary energy. Iceland as a moody, cinematic experience. Maximum contrast with v1.
v3

Magazine Editorial

CSS Grid asymmetric layout, bold Impact headers, red accents. The diary as a travel magazine spread. Confident, editorial, dense.
v4

Brutalist

Courier New, thick black borders, yellow highlights, broken grid. Anti-design as a design choice. Raw and confrontational.
v5

Split Screen

Fixed left image panel, scrollable right text. Swiss precision applied to a travel diary. Geometric, structured, calm.
v6

Vintage Journal

Parchment tones, drop caps, sepia images, ornamental rules. Reading a leather-bound travel journal found in an antique shop.
v7

Newspaper Broadsheet

Times New Roman, multi-column CSS, newsprint bg, masthead. Iceland as front page news. Formal, typographic, old-press energy.
v8

Vertical Timeline

Left-side timeline line, glowing dot markers, timestamped entries. The diary as a journey with a clear chronological spine.
v9

Typography Hero

15vw display text, full-bleed color blocks, images as tiny accents. What if type was the entire design? The words are the landscape.
v10

Photo Mosaic

All 6 images in a CSS grid filling the viewport, text overlaid with gradient. The drone footage speaks — text is secondary.
Phase 2 · v11–v19

Combine & Narrow

Taking the strongest ideas from Phase 1 and cross-pollinating them. The editorial structure from v3 plus the darkness of v2. The vintage warmth of v6 applied to the brutalist bones of v4. Patterns emerge.

v11

Minimal Editorial

v1's whitespace + v3's editorial grid. Two-column image/text sections, red section markers, restrained serif. More structure, same calm.
v12

Dark Journal

v2's darkness + v6's diary intimacy. Warm amber headings on near-black, journal voice in the dark. Aurora images glow warmer here.
v13

Bold Magazine

v3's editorial grid + v9's typographic scale. Giant red section headlines, multi-column body. High energy, visually loud.
v14

Warm Brutalist

v4's raw borders + v6's warm palette. Earthy terracotta borders, monospace in warm tones. Brutalism made approachable.
v15

Modern Broadsheet

v7's columns + v3's editorial sensibility. Cleaner than v7, more structured than v3. Column rules, bold sans headers, serif body.
v16

Dark Timeline

v8's timeline spine + v2's cinematic darkness. Glowing teal timeline dots, full-width image reveals, timestamps as waypoints.
v17

Cinematic Split

v5's split + v10's photo immersion. Full-height sections, alternating image/text sides, dark overlays. Immersive and cinematic.
v18

Typographic Vintage

v9's massive type + v6's vintage warmth. Large amber display text, ornamental unicode marks, parchment bg. Type as artifact.
v19

Photo Essay

v10's photo-first + v1's minimalism. Full-width images, white space, tiny captions. Text steps back. The drone lens tells the story.
Phase 3 · v20–v25

Converge & Refine

The chosen direction: a dark editorial journal — warm dark background, Georgia serif, gold accents, strong imagery. Each version adds one layer of refinement. v25 is the final answer.

v20

Core Direction

Establishing the final design language: dark warm bg, off-white serif text, full-width images. The foundation everything else builds on.
v21

Refined Typography

Modular type scale added, pull quotes per entry, tighter line-height on headings, letter-spacing on labels. Type starts breathing.
v22

Refined Spacing

Section padding 6rem, max-width 720px, generous margins between elements. The layout becomes intentional — not just good, but right.
v23

Refined Color

Gold accent #c8a96e locked in for dates/labels. Thin gold rules between entries. Image captions in muted warm gray. Palette settled.
v24

Refined Details

Site header with ICELAND in small caps, location/time image captions, gallery pill link, footer. Every element now has a reason.
v25 · FINAL

Final Version ★

Full-bleed hero with title overlay, entry number indicators in the margin, CSS custom properties throughout, every proportion deliberate. This is it.
